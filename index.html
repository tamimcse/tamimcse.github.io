MD Iftakharul Islam (Tamim)



I am a PhD candidate of Kent State University (Ohio, USA). I'm developing a software and ASIC (chip) based routing table lookup for core routers. I'm also interested in synthesizable Verilog generation from C programs (for ASIC). I previously worked on packet forwarding and TCP/IP stack of Linux kernel.

I had my bachelor degree from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). I worked as a software engineer for almost five years before starting gradschool. My CV and github.
