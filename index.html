I am a PhD candidate at computer science department of Kent State University. I am working on routing table lookup algorithms and high-level synthesis. I previously worked on packet forwarding and TCP/IP stack of Linux kernel.

I had my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering from BUET. I worked as a software engineer for five years before starting gradschool. My CV.
